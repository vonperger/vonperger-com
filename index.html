About me: interested in technology

Starting with a Computer Science degree at Oxford University, I have spent over 10 years in tech:

First as a strategy consultant within Mckinsey's Business Technology practice and afterwards building and running business development and operations teams at ecommerce and mobile consumer startups Stylistpick and Citymapper.

After 2 years as Principal at Venture Capital fund Wellington Partners, I finally took the leap of faith and started my first company: Zenjob, a mobile on-demand staffing platform.

In early 2017 I left Zenjob in the capable hands of my cofounders for it's next phase of growth and I am currently exploring new ideas for my next company. Get in touch!

In my spare time I write and run the European VC jobs newsletter.
